# IDEXX Color Palette Reference

This is the color palette Mel liked from IDEXX's branding that she wants to reference for her portfolio.

## Color Palette Analysis

Based on the theme colors image, the palette includes:

### Primary Colors (Top Row - Main Brand Colors)
- **Warm Gray/Tan**: Light neutral base
- **Charcoal Black**: Deep black for contrast and text
- **Light Gray**: Soft neutral
- **Navy Blue**: Deep, professional blue
- **Bright Blue** (circled in red): Vibrant blue accent - appears to be a key brand color
- **Emerald Green**: Rich jewel-tone green
- **Bright Cyan**: Fresh, modern cyan
- **Sky Blue**: Light, airy blue
- **Mint/Seafoam**: Soft green-blue
- **Plum/Burgundy**: Deep jewel-tone purple-red

### Supporting Gradients (Rows 2-5)
The palette shows tints/shades of the main colors, ranging from very light to very saturated:
- Grays (light to dark)
- Blues (light sky to deep navy)
- Greens (mint to emerald)
- Purples/Burgundy tones

## Recommended Palette for Mel's Portfolio

Based on Mel's stated preferences (jewel tones: green, turquoise, burgundy + grays and blacks) and this IDEXX palette:

### Primary Colors
- **Emerald Green**: #00A878 (estimated) - Rich, jewel-tone green
- **Bright Cyan/Turquoise**: #00BFFF (estimated) - Fresh, energetic
- **Plum/Burgundy**: #6B1F5C (estimated) - Deep, sophisticated accent

### Supporting Colors
- **Charcoal**: #2C2C2C (estimated) - For text and strong contrast
- **Medium Gray**: #6E6E6E (estimated) - For secondary text
- **Light Gray**: #E5E5E5 (estimated) - For backgrounds
- **Navy Blue**: #003D5C (estimated) - Professional depth
- **White**: #FFFFFF - Clean backgrounds

### Usage Recommendations
- **Primary CTA/Headers**: Emerald Green or Bright Cyan
- **Accent/Highlights**: Burgundy for important callouts
- **Text**: Charcoal on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds
- **Backgrounds**: White primary, Light Gray secondary, Navy for hero sections
- **Links/Interactive**: Bright Cyan or Emerald Green

## Design Notes
- The circled bright blue suggests this was a key accent color in IDEXX branding
- The palette has good range from light to dark for contrast
- Jewel tones provide richness without being overly playful
- Multiple blues and greens give flexibility while maintaining cohesion
