About Me

21 years of turning complex challenges into learning experiences that work

Hi, I'm Melissa

I'm a Learning Experience Designer who believes training should solve real problems, not just check boxes. After 21 years at IDEXX Laboratories, I've seen firsthand how the right learning solution can transform an organization. The results speak for themselves: reduced support costs, happier customers, and teams that feel empowered to do their best work.

My superpower? Taking complex, technical challenges and turning them into engaging learning experiences that actually stick. Whether it's migrating 800+ help files into a streamlined knowledge base (and cutting chat support by 94%), creating CE-certified eLearning courses, or redesigning an entire training program, I bring both strategic thinking and hands-on execution to every project.

I'm currently seeking new opportunities where I can bring this same results-driven approach to help organizations train smarter, not just more.

How I Work

My approach to creating learning experiences that deliver results

1

Understand the Problem

I start by asking the right questions. What business problem are we solving? Who are the learners? What does success look like?

2

Research & Analyze

I gather data through surveys, focus groups, and analytics. Understanding user pain points is key to designing effective solutions.

3

Design Strategically

Using ADDIE/SAM methodologies, I design solutions that fit the context: the right modality, the right depth, the right timing.

4

Build & Iterate

I develop content using tools like Articulate Rise, WalkMe, and Camtasia, incorporating feedback and refining as I go.

5

Measure Impact

Using the Kirkpatrick Model, I evaluate effectiveness and tie learning outcomes back to business metrics that matter.
