Content Strategy & Knowledge Management

The 94% Solution: Transforming Support Through Strategic Content

View Live Site →

The Challenge

IDEXX's on-premise veterinary software (Cornerstone) had accumulated 800+ legacy help files that were outdated, disorganized, and difficult to search. Support teams were overwhelmed with repetitive questions, and customers struggled to find answers quickly. Chat support volume was unsustainably high, impacting both customer satisfaction and operational costs.

My Approach

  • User Research: Conducted focus groups and surveys with customers and support technicians to identify pain points and information gaps
  • Content Audit: Categorized and assessed 800+ help files for relevance, accuracy, and user value
  • Information Architecture: Designed a new content structure optimized for findability and user workflows
  • Content Consolidation: Rewrote and consolidated content into 300 optimized knowledge base articles
  • Search Optimization: Implemented SEO best practices and improved metadata for better search results

The Results

94% Reduction in chat support volume
3-Year High Customer satisfaction (CSAT) score
3-Year Low Support case rates
62.5% Content reduction (800 to 300)

Content Strategy Information Architecture User Research WordPress Technical Writing Stakeholder Management Data Analysis

eLearning Development

CE-Certified Course Library: Scaling Training Through eLearning

The Challenge

IDEXX needed modern, engaging eLearning courses for both customers and internal teams to support their SaaS and on-premise software products. Traditional trainer-led sessions weren't scaling to meet demand, and courses needed to meet strict Continuing Education (CE) certification standards for veterinary professionals.

My Approach

  • Audience Analysis: Identified 4 unique user personas with different roles, goals, and learning needs
  • Curriculum Design: Applied ADDIE methodology to structure comprehensive learning paths
  • CE Compliance: Ensured courses met Continuing Education certification requirements for veterinary professionals
  • Role-Based Training: Created differentiated content paths for each user persona
  • Engagement Strategies: Incorporated interactive elements, knowledge checks, and real-world scenarios

The Results

15+ eLearning courses created
4 User personas served
CE Certified content
Scaled Training delivery

Skills Demonstrated

Articulate Rise Instructional Design (ADDIE) Bloom's Taxonomy Adult Learning Theory Persona Development CE Certification
Training Transformation

Training Program Redesign: From Bottleneck to Blended Learning

The Challenge

IDEXX's new hire training for customer support was 95% trainer-led, creating scheduling bottlenecks and limiting scalability. Additionally, troubleshooting training was delayed by nearly a year after hire, impacting employee development, retention, and service quality.

My Approach

  • Needs Analysis: Assessed current training effectiveness and identified opportunities for improvement
  • Blended Learning Design: Created a mix of virtual, in-person, self-paced, mentorship, and hands-on components
  • Curriculum Restructuring: Reorganized troubleshooting training to be delivered much earlier in the employee journey
  • Minimal Disruption: Designed implementation to minimize impact on call center operations
  • Quality Assurance: Established QA processes to maintain training effectiveness

The Results

11 Months Earlier troubleshooting training delivery
125+ Support technicians trained
Improved Employee retention
Increased Scheduling efficiency

Skills Demonstrated

Blended Learning Design Program Evaluation Change Management Needs Analysis Training Delivery Quality Assurance

WalkMe Implementation: In-App Learning That Meets Users Where They Are

The Challenge

Users of IDEXX's SaaS veterinary software were getting lost in complex workflows and features. They needed guidance at the moment of need, not just training they might forget later. Support teams were fielding repetitive "how do I...?" questions that could be prevented with better in-app guidance.

My Approach

  • KPI Tracking: Established metrics for measuring engagement and support reduction
  • Continuous Optimization: Iterated based on analytics and user feedback

Met User engagement KPIs

Improved User experience with just-in-time learning
Certified WalkMe Builder

Video Content Library: Visual Learning at Scale

The Challenge

Written documentation and eLearning courses weren't meeting the needs of all learners. Many customers and employees preferred video content for learning complex software workflows. IDEXX needed a library of professional instructional videos to support product releases and ongoing education.

My Approach

  • Scriptwriting: Developed clear, concise scripts optimized for visual demonstration

  • Accessibility: Added captions and ensured content met accessibility standards

The Results

Multi-Platform Video library (Wistia, YouTube, web)
Timely Support for product releases
Visual Learning format for all learner types
Professional Quality production

Train-the-Trainer Program: Multiplying Impact Through Others

The Challenge

IDEXX needed to scale training capacity beyond what a single training team could deliver. Subject matter experts had deep product knowledge but lacked the skills to effectively train others. Inconsistent training quality across different trainers was impacting learner outcomes.

My Approach

  • Program Design: Developed a comprehensive train-the-trainer curriculum
  • Facilitation Skills: Taught SMEs adult learning principles and facilitation techniques
  • Practice & Feedback: Created opportunities for practice teaching with constructive coaching
  • Quality Standards: Established consistent training standards and evaluation criteria
  • Ongoing Support: Provided mentoring and coaching as new trainers developed confidence

The Results

10 SMEs trained as trainers
75+ Field trainers led at summits
Scaled Training capacity organization-wide
Consistent Training quality achieved

Skills Demonstrated

Train-the-Trainer Mentoring Coaching Program Development Facilitation Leadership
